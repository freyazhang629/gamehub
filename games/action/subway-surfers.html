About Subway Surfers

Subway Surfers is the ultimate endless running game! Join Jake, Tricky & Fresh as they escape from the grumpy Inspector and his dog. Run as far as you can in this endless running adventure! Navigate the subway tracks, dodge oncoming trains, collect coins, and perform incredible acrobatic stunts.

Features exciting power-ups like the Jetpack, Super Sneakers, Coin Magnet, and more. Unlock new characters and hoverboards as you progress. With colorful HD graphics and smooth gameplay, Subway Surfers offers the ultimate mobile gaming experience.

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move left, right, jump, and slide
  • Spacebar: Activate power-ups
  • Mouse: Navigate menus and select items

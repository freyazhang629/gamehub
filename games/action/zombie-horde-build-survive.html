Game Description

Welcome to the ultimate zombie survival experience! In Zombie Horde: Build & Survive, you must use all your wits and skills to survive against endless waves of the undead. This intense action-survival game combines strategic building elements with fast-paced combat.

As a survivor in a post-apocalyptic world, you'll need to quickly construct defensive fortifications while fighting off increasingly dangerous zombie hordes. Scavenge for resources, craft powerful weapons, and build the ultimate base to protect yourself from the zombie apocalypse.

Key Features:

  • Intense Combat: Face various types of zombies with unique abilities and weaknesses
  • Base Building: Construct walls, barriers, and defensive structures
  • Weapon Crafting: Create and upgrade weapons using scavenged materials
  • Resource Management: Collect and manage limited resources strategically
  • Progressive Difficulty: Waves become more challenging with stronger zombies
  • Survival Mechanics: Monitor health, stamina, and ammunition
  • Horror Atmosphere: Immersive sound design and visual effects

Can you survive long enough to see the dawn? Test your survival skills in this thrilling zombie survival game!

How to Play

Movement

WASD Keys: Move your character around the map

Shift: Run (consumes stamina)

Combat

Mouse: Aim your weapon

Left Click: Fire weapon

R Key: Reload your weapon

Right Click: Melee attack (when close)

Building

TAB Key: Open building menu

1-9 Keys: Quick select building items

Left Click: Place selected structure

Right Click: Cancel building mode

Other Controls

E Key: Interact with objects/pick up items

ESC Key: Pause game/open menu

M Key: Toggle map view

Survival Tips
  • Start by building basic walls around your starting position
  • Always keep an eye on your ammunition count
  • Use melee weapons when ammunition is low
  • Collect resources during calm periods between waves
  • Upgrade your weapons as soon as possible
  • Create chokepoints to funnel zombies into kill zones
  • Don't forget to repair damaged structures

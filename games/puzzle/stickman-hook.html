About Stickman Hook

Stickman Hook is an awesome physics-based puzzle game where you control a stickman character swinging through challenging levels! Tap to hook and make incredible jumps as you avoid every obstacle that stands in your way. Master the physics of swinging to complete each level with style.

Execute acrobatic tricks in the air as you aim for the finish line. The more acrobatic your tricks, the more points you'll earn! With smooth animations and intuitive controls, this game offers hours of entertaining gameplay.

How to Play

  • Mouse/Touch: Click and hold to extend your hook and swing
  • Release: Let go to detach and fly through the air
  • Physics: Use momentum and timing to reach the goal
  • Tricks: Perform spins and flips for extra points
